About

I am web developer primary working with Ruby on Rails apps. I feel most comfortable in the application layer, but I also enjoy designing interfaces and writing bash scripts.

Background

My formal education consists of a Computer Information Systems degree from Chapman University in Orange, California (while also playing around with film production, accounting, and business). I must also give The Internet much due credit for my continued education in all things programming and design.

Philosophy

There is far too much sh**ty software out there. On behalf of all programmers everywhere, I apologize to those for whom technology has made their jobs more difficult rather than easier. In our defense, it's not easy to make usable, reliable, maintainable software; to have such an deep understanding of a problem that one is able to create instructions for a computer to carry out the solution. Personally, this challenge is precisely the reason I love to create software.
