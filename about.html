Philosophy

There is far too much sh**ty software out there. On behalf of all programmers everywhere, I apologize to all those for whom technology has made their jobs more difficult rather than easier. To our defense, it's not easy to make usable, reliable, maintainable software; to have such an deep understanding of a problem that you are able to create instructions for a computer to carry the solution. But for me, this challenge is precisely the reason I love to software development.

I strive for my applications to have both intuitive interfaces as well as powerful functionality. I will always take the extra effort to do it the Right way (time permitting) over the easy way.

Someone said this and I love it, "Design for the end users, develop for the next developer."
